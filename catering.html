Ordering Details

Condizione

Ordering & Deadlines

• Catering orders require a minimum notice of 72 business hours prior to the event date.

• Please advise us about any allergies when you place your order

• All catering orders must be submitted via email at catering@trufflebrothers.com or personally at our spaccio salumeria location


Payment Options

• A minimum 50% deposit is required upon confirmation of the order & the remaining 50% the day of the event


Serving & Staff

• All caterings will be served on plastic trays

• Plates & utensils are calculated based on serving size, no additional plates or utensils will be provided


Changes & Cancelations

• A fee may apply for any substitutions & customizations


Pick up & Delivery

• Delivery with a $200 minimum purchase & a fee of $10 per mile


For custom proposals or inquiries, please use the order form or email catering@trufflebrothers.com
